Everything a person does on a computer

No browser extension, no API wiring — Ember works your actual desktop.

Sees & clicks accurately

On-device OCR (Apple Vision on Mac, Windows OCR on PC) plus the accessibility tree locate on-screen text and click its exact center — no blind pixel guessing.

Top-tier reasoning

Runs on Gemini for free, with Claude Opus 4.8 — adaptive thinking and streaming — for the hard problems.

Real browser control

Drives Chrome/Edge over the DOM — fills forms, clicks links, reads pages — not just screenshots.

Phone remote

Ember Link mirrors your screen to your phone with a trackpad, keyboard, and a chat tab — over your Wi-Fi.

Hands-free voice

Continuous listen → act → speak turns, so you can just talk to your computer.

Asks before risky things

Destructive commands, sending data, and dangerous key-combos prompt for confirmation first.
